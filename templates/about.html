About Us

Welcome to AJET - Akungbe Journal of Economics Thought, a premier academic publication dedicated to advancing economic discourse and research.

AJET is a scholarly journal that provides a platform for economists, researchers, and academics to share cutting-edge research and theoretical developments in economics. Our journal focuses on publishing high-quality, peer-reviewed articles that contribute to economic thought and policy discussions. We welcome submissions across all areas of economics, including but not limited to macroeconomics, microeconomics, development economics, international economics, and economic policy analysis. As the flagship publication of Akungbe, we are committed to maintaining rigorous academic standards while fostering innovative economic thinking and research.
